QUESTIONS

What exercises does it support?

Any exercise that's visible in the video — squats, deadlifts, bench press, overhead press, rows, lunges, pull-ups, and more. The AI identifies the movement automatically.

How long should my video be?

Any length works. The smart motion detection finds the active portion of your video automatically, so you can hit record before you start your set and stop whenever.

Is my video stored anywhere?

No. Your video is processed entirely on your device. Only compressed image frames are sent for analysis, and those aren't stored either.

How much does it cost?

Each form check costs 15 credits. You get 50 free credits when you sign up, and earn more through training activities like completing workouts and hitting goals.

What camera angle works best?

A side view or 45-degree angle that shows your full body through the entire range of motion. Make sure the camera is stable and the lighting is reasonable.
